My favourite places in Bayern

Herzogstand

Herzogstand is a very beautiful place in the Alps not far from Minich. I used to go there for a weekend before. There are so many hiking routes and views there.

Viktualienmarkt

Viktualienmarkt is a daily food market and a square in the center of Munich. It's so nice to come there for a cup of coffee or a glass of wine and watch the market life.

Technische Universität München Cafe

Technische Universität München Cafe has a wonderful terrace.
